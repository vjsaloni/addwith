How it works

  • Spread the word

    Tell friends and family about Livspace and share their contact details with your designer to make a reference.

  • Earn

T&C's apply

  • 1. Valid only when the person you referred books a project with Livspace.
  • 2. Referral bonus is calculated on the total project value at the 50% payment stage.
  • 3. Not valid for internal Livspace teams.
  • 4. If your friend has already filled an enquiry form with Livspace, this referral will not be successful.
  • 5. Only ex/current customers can refer.
